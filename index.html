III.   The Story
Why We Built This

Pizza Deserves
Better.

Drip Austin started with a simple question: why does the best pizza in the world get served with the worst dipping sauce on earth? Industrial garlic spread, seed-oil ranch, sugar-loaded marinara. We thought pizza deserved better. So we built better.

From the test kitchen.

Every Drip sauce is built on olive oil and grass-fed ghee — never canola, never soybean, never sunflower. Real garlic, fresh herbs, single-origin chilis. The kind of ingredients you'd reach for if you were cooking for someone you love.

Made small-batch in Austin. Bottled by hand. Four launch flavors built to range from "my kid will eat it" to "finally, a ranch without seed oils." No corn syrup. No "natural flavors." No shortcuts.

We're a Woolf Ventures brand — small team, big standards, late nights in the kitchen.
